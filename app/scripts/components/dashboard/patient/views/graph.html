How to calculate adherence score?

{{fromDateHistory}} - {{toDateHistory}} {{toDateHistory}}
{{expandedSign}}
  • Everyone gets an Adherence Score of 100 to start
  • Every notification subtracts points from your Adherence Score
  • Notifications are based upon activity over the past three days
    • Missed Therapy Day Notification: 3 consecutive days with no therapy sessions recorded and notification continues each day until a therapy is recorded
    • Therapy Minutes Below Protocol: Total therapy minutes over the last 3 days is below protocol
    • Frequency Deviation Below Protocol: Average frequency is over 15% below protocol
  • Notification type and amount off your score are as follows:
    • Priority 1
  • Missed therapy days = 5 points off your score and lower priority notifications are ignored
    • Priority 2 (points deducted only if priority 1 notification is not active)
  • Therapy Minutes Notification = 2 points off your score
  • Device Frequency Notification = 1 point off your score
  • Every day you follow your care plan, 1 point is added (if below 100) back to your score
